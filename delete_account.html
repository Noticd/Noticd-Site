How to Delete Your Account

If you choose to delete your account, you can do it in one of two ways. 1) Directly in the app or 2) By contacting us from the email address associated with your account.

1. In-App Deletion

If you wish to delete your account through the app, follow these steps:

  1. Open the app
  2. Navigate to your profile through the bottom tab
  3. Click "Delete Account" under Settings
  4. Click continue and complete the confirmation flow

2. Email Deletion

If you can't access the app or want us to delete your account, follow these steps:

Send us an email at help.noticd.app@gmail.com. Title the subject line as "Account Deletion Request". We will verify your identity before deleting your account.

*Please contact us with the email address you accessed Noticd with.
