Noticd Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 18th, 2025

By using Noticd, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights under applicable privacy laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), and other applicable regulations.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Noticd, we may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: When you sign up or log in using Google or Apple, we receive your email address (or a private relay email) and basic profile information.
  • User Content: Text posts, replies, and user profiles you create/upload on the platform.
  • Device & Usage Information: Information about how you use the app (e.g., interactions, features accessed, crash reports, diagnostic data).

We do not collect video(s) or audio files.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Authenticate your account and keep it secure
  • Personalize your experience
  • Detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, or illegal activity

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information in the following limited ways:

  • Service Providers: With trusted vendors who help us operate, maintain, and improve the app (e.g., hosting providers, analytics services, crash reporting).

Aggregated & Anonymized Data: We may share aggregated, de-identified, and/or anonymized information that cannot reasonably be used to identify you. This may include:

  • Analytics: To help us understand how users interact with Noticd and improve our services.
  • Business-to-Business Partnerships: Providing aggregated and anonymized datasets for research, product improvement, or fine-tuning of large language models (LLMs) and related technologies.
  • Legal Requirements: If we are required to do so by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred to the new entity.

We never share personally identifiable information (such as your email or account details) with advertisers or external partners without your explicit consent.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations. When you request account deletion, we retain your data for a 30-day grace period to allow for account recovery, after which it is permanently deleted or anonymized.

5. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the following rights:

  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data (including account deletion).
  • Opt-out (California residents): Opt-out of the sale or sharing of your personal information (we do not currently share personal data).

You can exercise these rights by contacting us at help.noticd.app@gmail.com.

6. Children's Privacy

Noticd is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with data, contact us and we will delete it.

7. Data Transfers

If you access Noticd outside the United States, your information may be transferred and processed in countries with different data protection laws.

8. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If changes are significant, we will provide Notice (e.g., through the app or by email).
